Sarath Sivanacharya – Knitwear Designer & Textile Consultant

Sarath Sivanachaya is a passionate and forward-thinking knitwear designer and textile consultant with over six years of experience in the fashion and apparel industry. His journey began through event photography, where his eye for detail and creativity was first honed—skills that now fuel his innovative approach to textile design.
As the Founder of V Tex Syn, based in Tiruppur (India's knitwear capital), Sarath leads a team that manufactures custom t-shirts for over 50 corporate clients across India, USA, UAE and South Africa. His leadership has positioned V Tex Syndicate as a trusted partner in both manufacturing and consulting for emerging Indian knitwear brands.

Sarath’s quality-driven process ensures each piece reflects durability, precision, and market relevance. Beyond manufacturing, he mentors entrepreneurs and startup founders in the apparel space—blending practical industry experience with business strategy to support sustainable brand growth.

Whether you’re seeking high-quality custom apparel or expert guidance to build your fashion business, Sarath Sivanachaya is your trusted partner in the world of knitwear.
